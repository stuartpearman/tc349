ABOUT MY BLOG

Learnwithstu is basically a very show-offy blog centered around learning how to make things on the internet look really pretty. I made this blog so that one day I could be a great designer, but I think what could be even more valuable than my own tinkering is hearing what the rest of the world has to say. If this blog is exciting to you, or if your passionate about design as well, or even if you just want to talk for no good reason at all, get in touch with me, you'll see an excessive number of ways you can do this on the sidebar. I hope you enjoy the blog!

PLANS FOR THE FUTURE

I created this blog as a way to learn new things, and I have already learned a lot just in the initial set-up, but I think there's little point to learning if you're not teaching as well. I hope anyone who visits this blog gets something valuable out of it, educational or otherwise.
