NASA's MOON: CARD SORT

My sorting process

With NASA's well calculated site architecture already in front of me, it was tough to ignore that and come up with something entirely new, but having the physical cards in front of me helped force my brain into a new way of thinking.

I decided to group Moon Facts, The Moon in Song, Common Moon Misconceptions, and Products/ Merchandise together. My thought process for this was kind of a museum and gift shop theme: a few little known moon facts, as well as a place to buy merchandise to support the latest moon and space gear. This section is easy reading, and relatively entertaining without any hard science.

I put People, Neil Armstrong, and Strategic Partners together. This is kind of a "Meet the Team" section, with an homage to a past team member and pioneer of moon exploration.

I grouped Ladee Lunch, International Observe the Moon Night, Moon Toolkit, Features, NASA Lunar Science Forum, and Celebrate Your Moon together. These are all upcoming events, whether it's a particularly spectacular sighting of the moon, or a festival in celebration of the moon.

I put the interactive and social media modules together: Twitter Feed, News Feed, Calendar, Get Involved, Videos, and Images. This is kind of a profile page for the moon.

Lastly, I put the Past, Present, Future, and Conceptual Missions together. This is a good place to go if you want to learn a little history, as well as the current and future plans for moon exploration.
